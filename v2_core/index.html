About Me

I am a career changer from Math teacher to Full-stack Web Developer currently working as a Software Engineer with experience in the MEAN stack, Linux, Apache, Python and MySql. I seek to create innovative and practical websites that will fulfill the needs my clients. My analytical skills and attention to detail are my greatest assets within this career field and I hope to use them to grow my knowledge on a consistent basis.As an avid traveler who also possesses a passion for community activism, I hope to use my newly learned skills to support my areas of interest.
